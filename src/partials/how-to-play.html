How to Play

  • Swipe to Find Words:

    Swipe across the grid to discover hidden words, starting from any direction.

  • Unlock New Levels:

    Solve puzzles and advance through new, increasingly difficult levels.

  • Challenge Your Friends:

    Compete with friends to see who can find the most words in the shortest time.

  • Complete Daily Challenges:

    Test your skills with special challenges that give extra rewards.

  • Use Hints:

    Stuck? Use the hint feature to uncover difficult words and continue your progress.
